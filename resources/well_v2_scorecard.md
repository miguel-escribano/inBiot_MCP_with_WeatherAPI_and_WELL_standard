> Source: WELL Certification v2 Scorecard Q2-2025 (IWBI Official)
> Total Points Available: 110 (max 12 per concept, 100 across concepts + 10 Innovation)

| Level | Points Required |

| Silver | 50 points + all Preconditions |

## AIR CONCEPT (A01-A14)

### Preconditions (Mandatory)

#### A01 - Air Quality (P)
| Part | Parameter | Threshold | Verification |
|------|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| A01.1 | PM2.5 | ≤15 µg/m³ | Performance Test or Sensor |
| A01.1 | PM10 | ≤50 µg/m³ | Performance Test or Sensor |
| A01.2 | Formaldehyde | ≤50 µg/m³ | Lab Test |
| A01.2 | Benzene | ≤10 µg/m³ | Lab Test |
| A01.2 | Toluene | ≤300 µg/m³ | Lab Test |
| A01.2 | TVOC (alt) | ≤500 µg/m³ | Sensor Data |
| A01.3 | CO | ≤10 mg/m³ (9 ppm) | Performance Test or Sensor |
| A01.3 | Ozone | ≤100 µg/m³ (51 ppb) | Performance Test or Sensor |
| A01.4 | Radon | ≤0.15 Bq/L (4 pCi/L) | Sensor or LOA |
| A01.5 | Monitoring | Annual measurement of PM2.5, PM10, VOC/TVOC, CO, O3 | On-going Report |

#### A02 - Smoke-Free Environment (P)
- Indoor smoking prohibited
- Outdoor signage within 3m of entrances

#### A03 - Ventilation Design (P)
| Option | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Mechanical | ASHRAE 62.1 or equivalent |
| Natural | ASHRAE 62.1 Natural Ventilation + outdoor PM2.5 <15 µg/m³ |
| CO2 Monitoring | ≤900 ppm OR ≤500 ppm above outdoor |

#### A04 - Construction Pollution Management (P)
- Duct protection during construction
- MERV 8+ filters if HVAC operating
- Dust and moisture management

### Optimizations (Points)

#### A05 - Enhanced Air Quality (O)
| Part | Parameter | Tier 1 | Tier 2 | Points |
|------|-----------|--------|--------|--------|
| A05.1 | PM2.5 | ≤12 µg/m³ | ≤10 µg/m³ | 1-2 |
| A05.1 | PM10 | ≤30 µg/m³ | ≤20 µg/m³ | 1-2 |
| A05.2 | Formaldehyde | ≤9 µg/m³ | - | 1 |
| A05.2 | Benzene | ≤3 µg/m³ | - | 1 |
| A05.3 | CO | ≤7 mg/m³ (6 ppm) | - | 1 |
| A05.3 | NO2 | ≤40 µg/m³ (21 ppb) | - | 1 |

#### A06 - Enhanced Ventilation Design (O)
| Option | Requirement | Points |
|--------|-------------|--------|
| Increased supply | +30% / +60% outdoor air | 1-2 |
| DCV | CO2 ≤900/750 ppm | 1-2 |
| Monitoring | CO2 ≤750 ppm or ≤350 ppm above outdoor | 2 |

#### A07 - Operable Windows (O)
- 75% of spaces with operable windows
- Window operation indicators (PM2.5 ≤15, Temp within 15°F, RH ≤65%)

#### A08 - Air Quality Monitoring and Awareness (O)
- Monitors measuring ≥3 parameters (PM, CO2, CO, O3, NO2, TVOC, HCHO)
- Data displayed to occupants (screens or app)
- Calibration every 3 years

#### A09-A14 - Additional Features
- A09: Pollution Infiltration Management
- A10: Combustion Minimization
- A11: Source Separation
- A12: Air Filtration (MERV 12-16 based on outdoor PM2.5)
- A13: Enhanced Supply Air (100% outdoor or purification)
- A14: Microbe and Mold Control (UV treatment)

## THERMAL COMFORT CONCEPT (T01-T09)

### Preconditions (Mandatory)

#### T01 - Thermal Performance (P)
| Condition | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| Mechanical | PMV ±0.5 for 90% of hours in 90% of spaces |
| Natural | Adaptive model per ASHRAE 55 |
| Alternative | Dry bulb 70-77°F, air velocity ≤40 fpm |
| Commercial Kitchen | Operative temp ≤80°F |

**Monitoring (T01.2):**
- Semi-annual testing (summer + winter) OR
- Continuous monitoring per T06

### Optimizations (Points)

#### T02 - Verified Thermal Comfort (O)
| Tier | Satisfaction | Points |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 1 | 80% occupants satisfied | 2 |
| 2 | 90% occupants satisfied | 3 |

#### T03 - Thermal Zoning (O)
| Tier | Zone Size | Points |
|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | 650 ft² or 10 occupants | 1 |
| 2 | 320 ft² or 5 occupants | 2 |

#### T04 - Individual Thermal Control (O)
- Personal cooling options (fans, AC, etc.)
- Personal heating options (heaters, blankets)
- Flexible dress code policy

#### T05 - Radiant Thermal Comfort (O)
- Radiant heating (≥50% area)
- Radiant cooling (≥50% area)

#### T06 - Thermal Comfort Monitoring (O)
- Temperature and humidity sensors
- Data displayed to occupants
- Updates every 15 minutes

#### T07 - Humidity Control (O)
| Option | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Mechanical | 30-60% RH capability |
| Modeling | 30-60% RH for 98% of hours |
| Sensor Data | 30-60% RH during occupied hours |

#### T08-T09 - Beta Features
- T08: Enhanced Operable Windows
- T09: Outdoor Thermal Comfort

### Key Preconditions
- W01: Turbidity ≤1.0 NTU, no coliforms
- W02: Chemical thresholds (Lead ≤0.01 mg/L, etc.)
- W03: Legionella management plan

## SOUND CONCEPT (S01-S09)

### Key Requirements
- S01: Acoustic zone mapping (loud/quiet/mixed/circulation)
- S02: Background noise limits (35-55 dBA by room type)
- S03: Sound barriers (STC 40-60 by wall type)
- S04: Reverberation time (0.6-1.5s by room type)

## Compliance Calculation Rules

1. **90% Rule for Continuous Monitoring:**
   - Parameters must meet thresholds for ≥90% of occupied hours
   - Calculated over rolling periods (typically 30 days)

2. **Sampling Density:**
   - See `well_performance_verification.md` for detailed requirements

3. **Certification Level Limits:**
   - Modified PM thresholds (polluted regions): Max GOLD
   - Dynamic PM thresholds: Max SILVER

4. **Data Submission:**
   - Annual reporting through WELL digital platform
   - Calibration proof every 3 years
