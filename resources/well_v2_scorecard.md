# AIR CONCEPT (A01-A14) - DETAILED

## A01 - Air Quality (Precondition)

### A01.1 Meet Thresholds for Particulate Matter

**All Spaces except Commercial Kitchen & Industrial:**

| Option | PM2.5 | PM10 | Certification Limit | Verification |
|--------|-------|------|---------------------|--------------|
| 1: Acceptable | ≤15 µg/m³ | ≤50 µg/m³ | No limit | Performance Test or Sensor |
| 2: Modified (polluted) | ≤25 µg/m³ | ≤50 µg/m³ | Max GOLD | Performance Test or Sensor |
| 3: Dynamic (polluted) | ≤30% outdoor | ≤30% outdoor | Max SILVER | Performance Test or Sensor |

*Option 2-3: For buildings where annual outdoor PM2.5 ≥35 µg/m³*

**Commercial Kitchen & Industrial:**

| Option | PM2.5 | Certification Limit |
|--------|-------|---------------------|
| 1: Acceptable | ≤35 µg/m³ | No limit |
| 2: Dynamic (polluted) | ≤30% outdoor | Max SILVER |

### A01.2 Meet Thresholds for Organic Gases

**All Spaces:**

| Option | Requirements | Verification |
|--------|--------------|--------------|
| 1: Lab VOC tests | Benzene ≤10 µg/m³, Formaldehyde ≤50 µg/m³, Toluene ≤300 µg/m³ | Performance Test |
| 2: TVOC monitoring | Total VOC ≤500 µg/m³ | Sensor Data |

### A01.3 Meet Thresholds for Inorganic Gases

| Space Type | CO | Ozone |
|------------|-----|-------|
| All except Commercial Kitchen & Industrial | ≤10 mg/m³ (9 ppm) | ≤100 µg/m³ (51 ppb) |
| Commercial Kitchen & Industrial | ≤34 mg/m³ (30 ppm) | ≤100 µg/m³ (51 ppb) |

### A01.4 Meet Thresholds for Radon

**All Spaces - Choose one:**

| Option | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| 1: Radon testing | ≤0.15 Bq/L (4 pCi/L) on lowest occupiable floor. 1 test per 25,000 ft² |
| 2: Mechanical ventilation | Spaces at/below grade meet A03.1 Option 1 |
| 3: Above-grade | Located on/above 3rd floor OR raised-pier foundations |

### A01.5 Measure Air Parameters

**All Spaces except Dwelling Units:**

Annual monitoring required for:
- PM2.5
- PM10
- TVOC OR (Benzene + Formaldehyde + Toluene)
- Carbon Monoxide
- Ozone

Results submitted annually through WELL digital platform.

## A05 - Enhanced Air Quality (Optimization)

### A05.1 Meet Enhanced Thresholds for Particulate Matter

| Tier | PM2.5 | PM10 | Points |
|------|-------|------|--------|
| 1 | ≤12 µg/m³ | ≤30 µg/m³ | 1 |
| 2 | ≤10 µg/m³ | ≤20 µg/m³ | 2 |

### A05.2 Meet Enhanced Thresholds for Organic Gases

All thresholds must be met:
- Acetaldehyde: ≤140 µg/m³
- Acrylonitrile ≤5 µg/m³ OR Caprolactam ≤2.2 µg/m³
- Benzene: ≤3 µg/m³
- Formaldehyde: ≤9 µg/m³
- Naphthalene: ≤9 µg/m³
- Toluene: ≤300 µg/m³

**Points:** 1 | **Verification:** Performance Test

### A05.3 Meet Enhanced Thresholds for Inorganic Gases

- Carbon monoxide: ≤7 mg/m³ (6 ppm)
- Nitrogen dioxide: ≤40 µg/m³ (21 ppb)

**Points:** 1 | **Verification:** Performance Test or Sensor

## A07 - Operable Windows (Optimization)

### A07.1 Provide Operable Windows
- 75% of spaces with operable windows OR
- Openable window area ≥4% of floor area per floor

### A07.2 Manage Window Use
- Outdoor monitoring (PM2.5, temp, humidity) hourly within 2.5 mi
- Window indicators showing when to open: PM2.5 ≤15 µg/m³, Temp within 15°F, RH ≤65%

---

## A08 - Air Quality Monitoring and Awareness (Optimization)

### A08.1 Install Indoor Air Monitors

**All Spaces except Dwelling Units & Guest Rooms:**

Monitors measuring ≥3 of:
- PM2.5 or PM10
- CO2
- CO
- O3
- NO2
- TVOC
- Formaldehyde

Calibration/replacement proof every 3 years.

### A08.2 Promote Air Quality Awareness

- Display screens at 3.6-5.6 ft height, 1 per 5,400 ft² OR
- Website/app with signage indicating access
- Data updated every 15 minutes
- Show concentrations or color-coded levels

---

## A09 - Pollution Infiltration Management (Optimization)

### A09.1 Design Healthy Entryways
- Entry systems (grilles, mats, carpet tiles) spanning entrance width × 10 ft
- Air movement control (vestibule, revolving doors, air curtains)
- Weekly wet-cleaning, daily vacuuming

### A09.2 Perform Envelope Commissioning
- Façade engineer defines envelope performance
- Commissioning for air infiltration/leakage

---

## A10 - Combustion Minimization (Optimization)

### A10.1 Manage Combustion
- No indoor combustion appliances
- Low-emission equipment (SCAQMD compliant, electric, or district)
- No vehicle idling >30 seconds

---

## A11 - Source Separation (Optimization)

### A11.1 Manage Pollution and Exhaust
- Bathrooms, kitchens, chemical storage: self-closing doors or negative pressure
- Exhaust vented outdoors, not recirculated

---

## A12 - Air Filtration (Optimization)

### A12.1 Implement Particle Filtration

| Outdoor PM2.5 | Filter Efficiency |
|---------------|-------------------|
| ≤23 µg/m³ | ≥35% (MERV 12, M6) |
| 24-39 µg/m³ | ≥75% (MERV 14, F8) |
| ≥40 µg/m³ | ≥95% (MERV 16, E10) |

Annual filter replacement documentation required.

---

## A13 - Enhanced Supply Air (Optimization)

### A13.1 Improve Supply Air

| Option | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| 1: 100% outdoor | No recirculated air |
| 2: Purification | 2+ technologies: carbon filter, MERV 14+, UVGI. Zero ozone. |

### A13.2β Clean Airflow for Infectious Aerosols
- ASHRAE 241-2023 or 5 e/ACH
- Infection Risk Management Mode plan

## A14 - Microbe and Mold Control (Optimization)

### A14.1 UV Treatment for HVAC
- UV lamps on cooling coils and drain pans
- Annual lamp replacement/maintenance
- Quarterly coil inspection if no UV

# THERMAL COMFORT CONCEPT (T01-T09) - DETAILED

## T01 - Thermal Performance (Precondition)

### T01.1 Provide Acceptable Thermal Environment

**All Spaces except Commercial Kitchen:**

| Option | Requirement | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| 1: PMV | PMV ±0.5 for 90% of hours in 90% of spaces | LOA Engineer + Test |
| 2: Long-term data | T06 monitoring + (PMV range OR 70-77°F, ≤40 fpm) | Sensor Data |
| 3: Surveys | Achieve ≥2 points in T02 | Survey |

**Naturally conditioned spaces:** Adaptive model per ASHRAE 55
- Min indoor temp: tpma(out) × 0.31 + 47.9°F (when outdoor ≥50°F)
- Max indoor temp: tpma(out) × 0.31 + 60.5°F (when outdoor ≤92°F)

**Commercial Kitchen:** Operative temp ≤80°F (27°C)

### T01.2 Measure Thermal Parameters

| Option | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| 1: Semi-annual | Measure temp, RH, air speed, MRT twice/year (summer + winter) |
| 2: Continuous | Meet T06 Thermal Comfort Monitoring |

---

## T02 - Verified Thermal Comfort (Optimization)

### T02.1 Survey for Thermal Comfort

Post-occupancy survey twice/year (summer + winter):

| Occupants | Min Responses |
|-----------|---------------|
| >45 | 35% |
| 20-45 | 15 |
| <20 | 80% |

| Tier | Satisfaction | Points |
|------|--------------|--------|
| 1 | 80% satisfied | 2 |
| 2 | 90% satisfied | 3 |

---

## T03 - Thermal Zoning (Optimization)

### T03.1 Provide Thermostat Control

| Tier | Zone Size | Points |
|------|-----------|--------|
| 1 | 650 ft² or 10 occupants | 1 |
| 2 | 320 ft² or 5 occupants | 2 |

Requirements:
- Thermostat or digital interface in each zone
- Sensors ≥3.3 ft from walls, windows, diffusers, heat sources

---

## T04 - Individual Thermal Control (Optimization)

### T04.1 Personal Cooling Options
- Personal thermostat, desk/ceiling fan, cooling chair
- Must achieve PMV change of -0.5 within 15 min

### T04.2 Personal Heating Options
- Personal thermostat, space heater, heated chair, blankets
- Must achieve PMV change of +0.5 within 15 min

### T04.3 Flexible Dress Code
- Policy allowing occupants to dress for thermal preferences

---

## T05 - Radiant Thermal Comfort (Optimization)

### T05.1 Radiant Heating
- ≥50% of area heated by radiant ceilings, walls, floors, or panels

### T05.2 Radiant Cooling
- ≥50% of area cooled by radiant ceilings, walls, floors, or panels

---

## T06 - Thermal Comfort Monitoring (Optimization)

### T06.1 Monitor Thermal Environment

Requirements:
- Monitor dry-bulb temperature and relative humidity
- Per Continuous Monitoring Protocols in PVG
- Data displayed via screens (1 per 5,400 ft²) or app
- Updates every 15 minutes

---

## T07 - Humidity Control (Optimization)

### T07.1 Manage Relative Humidity

| Option | Requirement | Verification |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| 1: Mechanical | System capable of 30-60% RH | LOA Engineer |
| 2: Modeling | 30-60% RH for 98% of hours | Technical Doc |
| 3: Sensor data | 30-60% RH during occupied hours (requires T06) | Sensor Data |

---

## T08β - Enhanced Operable Windows (Beta)

### T08.1 Multiple Opening Modes
- 70% windows openable at ≤5.9 ft height
- 30% windows openable at ≥5.9 ft height (if heating present)
- Controls ≤5.6 ft above floor

---

## T09β - Outdoor Thermal Comfort (Beta)

### T09.1 Manage Outdoor Heat
- 50% pedestrian paths shaded
- 25% parking shaded
- 25% plazas/seating shaded

### T09.2 Avoid Excessive Wind
- CFD modeling: winds ≤11 mph for 95% of hours in seating areas

# Compliance Rules

1. **90% Rule:** Parameters must meet thresholds for ≥90% of occupied hours
2. **Certification Limits:**
   - Modified PM thresholds (polluted): Max GOLD
   - Dynamic PM thresholds: Max SILVER
3. **Calibration:** Every 3 years for continuous monitors
4. **Data Submission:** Annual through WELL digital platform
